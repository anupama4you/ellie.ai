# Ellie — AI Receptionist for Small Business

Ellie answers every call your business receives — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — books appointments, and sends SMS confirmations, all without you lifting a finger.

---

## The Problem

Small businesses miss calls constantly. A customer rings during a treatment, a job, a lunch break, or after hours. They don't leave a voicemail. They call the next business on Google.

Research puts the average revenue loss from missed calls at **$2,400 per month** for a typical small business. Ellie fixes that.

---

## What Ellie Does

**Answers calls in under 2 rings**
Ellie picks up immediately — no hold music, no voicemail, no missed opportunities. She sounds like a real receptionist, not a robot.

**Books appointments**
Ellie collects the caller's name, phone number, preferred date and time, and reason for the call. She confirms the booking back to them and fires off an SMS confirmation.

**Handles after-hours enquiries**
Calls at 10pm? Ellie answers, takes their details, and makes sure the team has everything they need first thing in the morning.

**Learns your business automatically**
Enter your website URL and Ellie reads your services, hours, location, and pricing. She's briefed and ready to represent your business in under 60 seconds.

**Manages the full call — start to finish**
Greetings, FAQs, booking capture, angry callers, awkward pauses — Ellie handles the entire conversation. If she genuinely can't answer something, she takes a message and flags it for you.

## How a Call Works

1. A customer calls your business number
2. Ellie answers within 2 rings
3. She greets them using your business name and handles their enquiry
4. If it's a booking — she collects their details and locks it in the calendar
5. The customer gets an SMS confirmation
6. You get a full call summary in your dashboard

The customer often can't tell it's AI. That's the point.
