About Ellie

What Ellie does, and how she uses your data.

Ellie is an AI phone receptionist service for small businesses, operated by Anupama Dilshan Withanage (ABN 11 685 248 475), South Australia, Australia. Ellie answers incoming calls 24/7 using AI voice technology, handles customer enquiries, books and reschedules appointments, and sends SMS confirmations — so a business never misses a call or a customer.

When a business chooses to connect their Google Calendar, Ellie requests calendar access solely to check real-time appointment availability during a call and to create the resulting booking as a calendar event on the business's behalf. Ellie never reads unrelated events or shares calendar data with third parties, and access can be disconnected at any time from the dashboard.
